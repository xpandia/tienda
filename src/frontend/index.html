How It Works

Three steps. Zero blockchain.

If you can post on Instagram, you can open your store on-chain.

1

Open your store

Sign up with your phone number. Choose a name for your store. In 30 seconds you have a deployed smart contract and a unique URL.

⚡ CosmWasm + Account Abstraction
2

Add products

Take a photo, set a price, and publish. Products are stored on-chain with images on IPFS. Atomic inventory via Move.

📷 Move Modules + IPFS
3

Sell and get paid

Share your link. Buyers pay with any token. You receive in your preferred currency. Instant settlement via Initia.

💰 Initia Enshrined Liquidity + IBC
