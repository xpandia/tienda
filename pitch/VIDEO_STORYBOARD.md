# TIENDA — Video Storyboard (60-90 seconds)

### INITIATE: The Initia Hackathon Season 1

---

## Video Specs

- **Duration:** 75 seconds (target)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (landscape)
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum
- **Audio:** Voiceover + subtle background music (upbeat, Latin-influenced lo-fi)
- **Tone:** Confident. Warm. Revolutionary but grounded.
- **Style:** Clean motion graphics + real device footage. No stock photos of "diverse teams shaking hands."

## Production Notes

### Music
- Style: upbeat lo-fi with Latin American influence (acoustic guitar, light percussion)
- Avoid: corporate synth, dubstep drops, anything that screams "crypto bro"
- Suggestion: browse Epidemic Sound or Artlist, filter by "Latin", "Warm", "Inspiring"
- Volume: 20-30% under voiceover, 80% during visual-only moments

### Voiceover
- Language: English (for hackathon judges), with Spanish product names pronounced naturally
- Tone: calm confidence, not hype. Think documentary narrator, not infomercial host
- Pace: deliberate. Let the sentences breathe. Silence is a tool.

### Screen Recordings
- Record on actual device at 60fps
- Use a clean, minimal phone case (or no case)
- Tap deliberately -- judges need to see every interaction
- Pre-load all data so there are no loading spinners in the video

### Color Grading
- Warm tones for real-world footage
- Clean whites and the Tienda brand palette for UI footage
- High contrast text overlays -- must be readable at 720p

### Titles and Text
- Font: clean sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or similar)
- Animate in with purpose -- no gratuitous motion
- Every word on screen must earn its place
