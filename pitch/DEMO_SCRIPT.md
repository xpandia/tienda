# TIENDA — 3-Minute Demo Script

### INITIATE: The Initia Hackathon Season 1

---

## Pre-Demo Setup

- Phone with Tienda app open (or emulator)
- Browser with Initia testnet explorer in background
- Store link ready to show buyer experience
- Timer visible: 3:00

---

## [0:00 - 0:25] THE HOOK

**[Speaker on camera, no screen share yet]**

> "Meet Maria. She makes the best handmade leather bags in Bogota. She sells on Instagram. Her customers DM her, she screenshots prices, they wire money to her bank, and she prays the payment clears before she ships.
>
> MercadoLibre wants 25% of every sale. Shopify does not support her currency. And Web3? Web3 wants her to understand seed phrases.
>
> Maria deserves better. Let me show you what better looks like."

**[Switch to screen share -- phone screen]**

---

## [0:25 - 1:05] ACT 1: MERCHANT ONBOARDING (40 seconds)

**[Phone screen: Tienda app, sign-up screen]**

> "Step one. Maria downloads Tienda and signs up with her phone number."

**[Tap: enter phone number, receive SMS code, verify]**

> "No seed phrase. No wallet setup. No MetaMask popup. Just a phone number -- the same way she signed up for WhatsApp."

**[Tap: enter store name "Maria's Leather" -- store is created]**

> "Her store is live. And what just happened behind the scenes is remarkable -- watch."

**[Switch to browser: Initia testnet explorer showing the transaction]**

> "A sovereign storefront contract just deployed on the Tienda rollup. Maria has her own on-chain store. She has no idea. She does not need to."

**[Switch back to phone]**

---

## [1:05 - 1:45] ACT 2: ADD A PRODUCT (40 seconds)

> "Step two. Maria adds her first product."

**[Tap: "Add Product" button]**

> "She takes a photo of her bag..."

**[Tap: camera icon, select photo from gallery]**

> "...types a name, sets a price in Colombian pesos..."

**[Type: "Crossbody Bag - Camel", Price: 180,000 COP]**

> "...and taps publish."

**[Tap: Publish button -- product appears in store]**

> "That photo is now pinned to IPFS. The product metadata -- price, inventory count, description -- is stored on-chain via a Move module. Atomic. Immutable. Verifiable."

**[Quick flash to explorer: show the Move module transaction]**

> "But Maria just sees her product in her store. That is the point."

---

## [1:45 - 2:30] ACT 3: THE PURCHASE (45 seconds)

> "Now let us be the buyer. Maria shares her store link on Instagram."

**[Switch to second device or browser tab: open store link]**

> "Her customer clicks the link. No app download required -- it is a progressive web app. Look at this storefront."

**[Show the buyer-facing store: clean, product grid, Maria's branding]**

> "They tap the bag. They tap Buy. And here is where it gets interesting."

**[Tap: Buy Now]**

> "The buyer can pay with any supported token. Stablecoins, INIT, whatever they hold. Initia's enshrined liquidity handles the conversion."

**[Select payment method, confirm purchase]**

> "Transaction confirmed. Sub-second. And look--"

**[Switch back to Maria's phone: order notification appears]**

> "Maria gets a push notification. The order is in. And the funds? Already settled. Not in 14 days. Not in 7 days. Now."

**[Show merchant dashboard: balance updated, order details visible]**

> "1.5% transaction fee. That is it. Compare that to the 25% she was paying before."

---

## [2:30 - 2:50] ACT 4: THE POWER UNDERNEATH (20 seconds)

> "But we did not just build a store. We built a chain."

**[Switch to explorer: show the Tienda rollup overview]**

> "Every store is a contract. Every product is on-chain state. Every review is verifiable. Every loyalty point is a programmable token. And because this is Initia -- every merchant on Tienda is natively connected to every other rollup in the ecosystem via IBC."

> "A buyer on a gaming rollup can purchase from Maria without ever leaving their chain."

---

## [2:50 - 3:00] THE CLOSE

**[Speaker on camera]**

> "300 million consumers. Millions of merchants. Zero blockchain jargon.
>
> This is Tienda. Tu tienda. Tu cadena. Tu futuro.
>
> Built on Initia. Built for Latin America. Built this week."

**[End]**

---

## Demo Checklist

- [ ] Phone (or emulator) charged and on Do Not Disturb
- [ ] Testnet funded with test tokens
- [ ] Product photo pre-loaded in gallery (for speed)
- [ ] Initia explorer tabs pre-loaded
- [ ] Buyer store link ready in second browser tab
- [ ] Screen recording running as backup
- [ ] Internet connection tested (use hotspot as fallback)
- [ ] Practice run completed in under 2:50 (leave 10s buffer)

---

## Backup Plan

If live demo fails at any point:
1. Switch to pre-recorded screen capture of the same flow
2. Narrate over it with the same script
3. Never apologize for the tech -- pivot smoothly: "Let me show you what this looks like..."

**Rule: The demo must feel effortless. If it does not, it is not ready.**
